Spoiled covers up all text and images containing terms you provide.

Block Anything

Type any phrase to block it from websites you browse. You aren't limited by specific shows or presents. Spoiled was designed to make adding and removing phrases as fast and easy as possible.

Free and Open Source

I created Spoiled because I didn't trust that other blockers were handling my data with care. Many even ask for social network feed access which always felt pretty invasive for something as simple as blocking the latest Game of Thrones spoiler.

Spoiled is entirely open source and collects no data of any kind, so the terms you enter or the sites you browse are known only to you.
